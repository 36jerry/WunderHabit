{% trans "Privacy" %}

{% url 'contact' as contact_url %} {% blocktrans %}

The purpose of this site is to inform you about which data we are storing on the server when you connect your Wunderlist and Habitica account. There is nothing fancy going on, but as a WunderHabit user, we think it is your right to know what is happening with your data.

Maybe the most important thing to say is, that none of your data is shared with any other third party. Basically, everything you see in your dashboard is stored on our server and is never shared with others. If you delete your account, all data related to you will be deleted.

If you do not agree with our privacy policy, you can always delete your account together with your personal data. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions.

{% endblocktrans %}

{% trans "Wunderlist" %}

{% blocktrans %}

By connecting your Wunderlist account, WunderHabit receives a user object containing your email address, your full name, your user ID and an API access token. This personal information is stored on our server as long as your account is active, and deleted together with your account if you choose to do so.

When connecting a list with WunderHabit, a webhook is registered to receive updates on the list. The only thing stored related to your connected lists is the name of the list, a count of your completed To-Do's (no information about the individual tasks), the ID of the list and the ID of the webhook to de-register it if necessary. No information about not connected lists is stored.

{% endblocktrans %}
